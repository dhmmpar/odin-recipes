Chocolate cake recipe

This Skip the store-bought mix and make this one-bowl chocolate cake from scratch

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stir ingredients
  2. Add milk and vanilla extract
  3. Stir in boiling water
  4. Pour into pans
  5. Bake
